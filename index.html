About Us 💚

Silent Cry of Earth is a community project that encourages people to take real action to help the environment while earning verified volunteer hours.

💚 Small actions like picking up trash, planting trees, or saving water can create a much bigger impact when shared with others. 🌎 Many people already do good things for the planet but don’t record or spread awareness, which limits how much change we can make together.

📸 By showing your actions through short videos, you help inspire others and build a community that truly makes a difference. ✨ Our goal is to help you protect the environment while reaching a wider audience and creating lasting change.

🪜 Small steps lead to BIG change.
Let’s heal the Earth together. 🌿✨

How to Volunteer 🌍

To earn volunteer hours, you must:
✅ Help the environment in a visible way
🎥 Film a 1–2 minute video (before/after preferred)
📝 Submit your video via Instagram DMs
📋 Fill out the Google Form below for verification
