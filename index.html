About Us 💚

SilentCry of Earth is a community project that encourages people to take real action to help the environment while earning verified volunteer hours.

💚 This program matters because small changes — like picking up trash, planting trees, or saving water — can create a much bigger impact when shared with others.

🌎 Many people already do good things for the planet but don’t record or spread awareness, which limits how much change we can make together.

📸 By showing your actions through short videos, you help inspire others and build a community that truly makes a difference.

✨ Our goal is to help you protect the environment while reaching a wider audience and creating lasting change. That’s what we’re all about: making small changes grow into big ones — but it can only go big WITH YOUR help 🫶.

🪜 Small steps lead to BIG change. Let’s make a change together🌿✨🫟

How to Volunteer 🌍

To earn volunteer hours, you must:
✅ Help the environment in a visible way
🎥 Film a 1–2 minute video (before/after preferred)
📝 Submit your video via Instagram DMs
