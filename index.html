About Us

Silent Cry of Earth is a youth-led environmental movement created to raise awareness about the quiet but urgent call of our planet. Our logo’s large “S” symbolizes the Silent Cry of nature, surrounded by leaves representing growth and unity. We aim to inspire people to act for the Earth through cleanups, sustainability efforts, and awareness campaigns.

Our Mission

We strive to give nature a voice through community engagement, volunteering, and environmental education. Our mission is to create awareness about climate change, pollution, and sustainability while encouraging people to adopt eco-friendly habits.

Join as a Volunteer

You don’t need to be an expert — just someone who cares. Join our volunteer community and take part in cleanups, tree-planting drives, and awareness campaigns.
